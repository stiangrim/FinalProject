Features

Easy access in and out of your gym.

View your number of visits to gym.

Show your average training time per session.

Compare with your friends.

Supports English and Norwegian.

Push notifications.

Supports iPhone, iPod Touch and iPad.

Supports Android 1.5 and above.


Never forget your key ever again

Think about it, what do you never leave your house without? Your cellphone of course! And how many times a week do you forget your gym key? With GymPal you would never have to think about bringing your access key to your gym again, because it is always with you! GymPal generates a new unique key every day, witch makes it safe for both the user and the gym. It is both quick and reliable, and supports all of the big gyms in Norway!




Track your activity and compete!

GymPal lets you track your activity in the gym, and all you have to do is to scan your key! Every time you scan your key, GymPal adds it to your statistics – and best of all, you can compare it to your friends! GymPal logs information as how many times you have visited the gym, how many sessions you average per week, and how many hours you have spent in the gym!

GymPal also lets you compare these statistics to your friends’ statistics, to see which one of you spends the most time in the gym! GymPal also has the possibility to track information for each one of your gym memberships – and to see which of your friends are working out right now! GymPal supports both Apple and Android devices, as well as it supports the largest gyms in Norway. Which makes it easy to connect with your friends, no matter where they workout!















Get statistics that interests you!

Wondering how long workouts you tend to have? Or your average gymsessions per week? GymPal knows the answer! Your workout start when you check in at the Gym, using your GymPal key, and ends when you check out. Based on this, we will give you all the info you need to track your workout plan.


You can see how long your sessions lasts, and how long your average sessions are. You will also know at what days and time you usually workout. You can also go back to previously weeks to compare.
